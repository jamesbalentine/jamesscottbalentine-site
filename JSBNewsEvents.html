Performances of Navarro, a children's opera in San Antonio area schools, May, 2012

The children's opera Navarro, commissioned by the Opera Guild of San Antonio, will be presented to elementary schools during the month of May, 2012, by the UTSA Lyric Theater under the direction of Dr. William McCrary.
